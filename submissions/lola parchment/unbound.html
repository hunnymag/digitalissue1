Unbound

by Lola Parchment

Every person embodies their own fusion of femininity and masculinity. However, it's worth rethinking what masculinity and femininity even mean. Fashion can be so restricting in who it allows to wear what. Unbound attempts to challenge the boundaries found in fashion and capture the beauty in being boundless.

This was taken with a 35mm film camera (Nikon FM10) using B&W Ilford HP5 film
